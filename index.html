Course Management System

The course Management System Caters to Any institution in managing the data.In an educational Institute there are several courses running parallel every day. For every course there are several batches at same/different time. Every course has a session wise/day wise schedule. It will be difficult for the Faculty Head to know the status of every batch i.e what is the session taught at each day in a batch.So the Automated Course Monitoring System helps keep the records of all the courses,batches,faculties and the daywise update for every batch. It also helps the Faculty Head to maintain the course plan.

Individual project: Completed in 5 days
